About Us

The Telecom ParisTech Debating Society

TELECOM ParisTech Debating Society is a club registered with the BDE ( Student Union) of our institution. Within the club, we mainly debate in British Parliamentary Style, but we enjoy French Debating Association style as well. Most of the members attend Mandy Weyer-Brown's class at TELECOM ParisTech, but it is not compulsory.

Thanks to the financial support from the BDE, the Society enables its members to take part in various European intervarsities debate competitions. Moreover, since 2004 we have participated in every tournament of the French Debating Association, which is a tournament opposing parisian "Grandes Ecoles" and a some universities every year. To finish with, it hosts the Paris Intervarsity, co-organised with ESSEC, Ponts and Centrale.

Join Us !

Any TELECOM Paristech student can join the Debating Society for free and can attend all debates, workshops. The society will pay your entry fees to the competitions it plans to take part in.
